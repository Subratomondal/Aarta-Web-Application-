Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2025

Our Commitment to Quality

At Aarta, our mission is to connect you with unique, handcrafted items from talented independent artists across India. Each piece is made with care, and we want you to be delighted with your purchase. This policy outlines the process for returns and refunds in the rare event that your order does not arrive as expected.

1. Return & Reporting Window

Please inspect your order immediately upon receipt. You have 24 hours from the time your order's tracking status is marked as "Delivered" to report any issues and request a return.

Important Note on Delivery: If the outer shipping package arrives visibly crushed, torn, or severely damaged, we strongly advise you to refuse the delivery from the courier. Please take a photo of the damaged package and contact our support team at subratomondal374@gmail.com immediately so we can resolve the issue with the artist and courier.

2. Valid Reasons for a Return

We accept returns only under the following conditions:

  • Item Arrived Damaged: The product itself (not just the outer packaging) is broken, cracked, or otherwise damaged.
  • Incorrect Item Received: You received a different product, color, or variant than what was listed on your order confirmation.

To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in the same condition you received it, and in its original packaging.

3. Non-Returnable Items

To protect the livelihood of our independent artists, we cannot accept returns for the following reasons:

  • "Change of Mind."
  • Custom-made or personalized orders.
  • Slight Variations: As all our products are handcrafted, slight variations in color, texture, or pattern are a natural part of the item's uniqueness and are not considered defects.

4. How to Initiate a Return

If your return request meets the criteria above, please follow these steps within the 24-hour window:

  1. Log in to your Aarta account and navigate to the "My Orders" page.
  2. Find the relevant order and click the "Request Return" button.
  3. Fill out the return request form, selecting a valid reason.
  4. Upload clear, well-lit photos showing the damage or the incorrect item. Return requests for damaged items without photographic evidence will not be processed.
  5. Submit the form. Your request will be sent to the artisan for review.

We will provide an update on the status of your request within two (2) business days.

5. The Refund Process

  1. Approval: If your return request is approved, you will receive instructions on how to return the item to the artisan.
  2. Return Shipping: For approved returns, the artisan is responsible for arranging and covering the cost of the return shipment.
  3. Inspection & Refund: After the artisan confirms receipt of the returned item, Aarta will process a full refund to your original payment method. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

6. Our Role as Mediator

Aarta acts as the marketplace and mediator. If there is a dispute between a buyer and an artisan regarding a return, our support team will review all evidence and make a final, binding decision.
